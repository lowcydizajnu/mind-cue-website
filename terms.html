2. Description of the service

Think Cue is a personal productivity and wellbeing application that:

  • Integrates with your device calendar to display your schedule
  • Attaches evidence-based behavioural tips to calendar events (breathing exercises, cognitive bias awareness, emotion-regulation techniques)
  • Provides configurable pre-event and post-event notifications
  • Offers a structured journaling feature based on the ABC cognitive-behavioural model
  • Tracks mood patterns and generates personal insights
  • Provides a curated library of learning content based on peer-reviewed research
  • Optionally uses AI (Claude by Anthropic) to classify events and personalise technique recommendations
